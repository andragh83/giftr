Another bad gift?

Remember all those times you had to buy a gift for someone and had absolutely no idea what to get? What about those times you got such a bad gift you stashed it somewhere and forgot where you put it?

Bad gifts = wasted money


Because the people who buy them spend (somethimes a considerable amount of) money on something that will be hated or will never be used.

But we have a solution!

We're building




An app that will solve the bad gifts problem once and for all.

Promise!

How it works?

First you browse online stores or go to street stores and find stuff you like. Sooo much fun, right?
Then you collect in GIFTR the stuff you like - from online stores by taging links and from street stores by tagging locations and adding photos.
Then you share youy GIFTR lists with loved ones or you add them event invitations like your birthday, wedding and so on. This way, when people want to you get you something, they will know exactly what to buy.
End of struggle for the buyer!! But especially problem solved for you! Because you'll get just what you wished for! Everyone's happy!
